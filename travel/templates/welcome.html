Executive Director

Mr. Ssejemba Kennedy Faustin



We are excited to introduce our tours and travel/religious pilgrimages and team-building organization. Our well-established firm is run by people who have worked in the travel industry for over 8 years. Different institutions have recognized our facilitators for offering cooperative team-building and experiential learning. We strive to provide our clients with high-quality, efficient services. We pride ourselves on being forward-thinking, inventive, and efficient, and we look forward to displaying these traits as possible. Thank you for taking the time to read this.

Over 20 years' experience working with both the private and not-for-profit sectors undertaking a range of interventions in business development, strategy formulation and review, research, project monitoring and evaluation, and capacity building in Uganda, Kenya, Rwanda, Burundi, Tanzania, South Sudan, Malawi, and Ethiopia. Kennedy has also lectured at various reputable institutions in Uganda in the areas of marketing, strategy, entrepreneurship, project monitoring & evaluation for the last 10 years. These institutions include: Uganda Management Institute, Makerere University, and Uganda Martyrs University. Kennedy is currently the Managing Director of 3A Strategic Management Consultants Ltd, a management consultancy firm based in Kampala with over 20 years' experience offering business development support services to both the private and not-for-profit sectors in Uganda and within Africa. Kennedy has a Doctor of Business Administration-Swiss School of Management (Switzerland), Master of Philosophy (Maastricht School of Management Netherlands), MBA (MUK), and a Bcom (MUK).
